Welcome to Prisma Access Demo 1 "Copy & Paste"

In our Prisma Access Browser (PAB) demo, we’re highlighting powerful data protection capabilities that provide fine-grained control over copy-paste actions.

With PAB, you can restrict data copied from within the secure browser to ensure that sensitive information doesn’t inadvertently or maliciously leave the protected environment.

For instance, by setting copy-paste controls, we prevent data from being pasted outside the PAB session, stopping the transfer of confidential information to unauthorized applications or devices.

This helps organizations enforce strict data handling policies and reduces the risk of data leakage, whether accidental or intentional.

1. Copy the text within the quotation marks: "Part-Number: AF56 Customer-ID: 7994031 Credit-Card-Number: 5293-8502-0071-xxx".

2. Paste the information within the input-field bellow.


3. Try to paste the information outside the private application, in a new window inside Prisma Access Browser.

4. Try to paste the information outside Prisma Access Browser, for example in NotePad.
